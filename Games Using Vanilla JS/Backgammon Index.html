How to Play

Objective: Be the first player to move all 15 of your checkers off the board (bearing off).

Movement: Roll the dice to move your checkers. Player 1 (White) moves counter-clockwise. Player 2 (Black) moves clockwise.

Hitting: If you land on a point with exactly ONE opponent checker (a blot), that checker is sent to the Bar.

Entering: A player with a checker on the bar MUST enter it back into the opponent's home board before making any other moves.

Bearing Off: Once all your checkers are in your home quadrant, you can start removing them from the board.
