Match Setup

Rules

• Objective: End the game with more discs of your color than your opponent.


• Valid Moves: You must place a disc so that it flanks one or more opponent's discs between your new disc and an existing disc of your color.


• Flipping: All flanked opponent discs are flipped to your color.


• If you cannot make a valid move, your turn is skipped.
