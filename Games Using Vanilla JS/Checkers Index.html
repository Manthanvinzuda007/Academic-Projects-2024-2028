Rules of Play

Objective: Capture all of your opponent's pieces or block them so they cannot make a move.

Movement: Pieces move diagonally forward one square at a time. Tap a piece to select it, then tap an highlighted valid cell to move.

Jumping: If an opponent's piece is diagonally adjacent and the square behind it is empty, you must jump over it to capture it. Chain jumps are mandatory if available.

Kings: When a piece reaches the farthest row, it becomes a King (Crown icon). Kings can move diagonally both forward and backward.

Time: Each player has 5 minutes on their clock. If your timer runs out, you lose!
