Rules of Play

Objective: Checkmate the opponent's King.

Movement: Tap a piece to see its valid moves highlighted with dots. Tap a highlighted square to execute the move.

Pawn: Moves forward 1 square (or 2 on first move). Captures diagonally.

Knight: Moves in an 'L' shape (2 squares one direction, 1 square perpendicular) and can jump over pieces.

Bishop/Rook/Queen: Move diagonally, straight, or both across empty squares.

King: Moves 1 square in any direction. The game ends immediately when a King is in Checkmate or Stalemate.
