How to Play

Objective: Be the first player to connect 4 of your colored discs in a row (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally).

Gameplay: On your turn, drop one of your discs into any of the 7 columns. The disc will fall to the lowest available empty slot within the column.

Winning: The game ends immediately when a player lines up 4 consecutive discs. If the board fills completely without a winner, the game is a draw.
